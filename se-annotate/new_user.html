Why do we ask you to use the same username and password as on StackExchange?
Because we are talking to StackExchange about feeding these annotations back into the website. That way the work you do here will actually show up on the website, instead of it only being of interest to the scientific community. It is important to StackExchange to only allow duplicate tags made by users with enough reputations points. The only way to check whether what you submit here matches your account, is by looking at the combination of username and email address. Any annotations made by a user that we cannot find in StackExchange, will not end up on the website. We can still use the annotations for our research, but the community will not benefit from it directly. That's why we're asking you to use the same username and email address as you use on StackExchange.
